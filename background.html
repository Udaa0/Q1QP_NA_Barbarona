Background

This dataset can reveal the results in performance among students from different universities or colleges. By examining the data closely, we can gain insights into how the university or college an individual is affiliated with may influence their academic outcomes.


These examinations were designed to determine the knowledge and skills of students, particularly in general management and their chosen domain-specific subjects. Each row in this dataset represents the unique performance of an individual student in the examination.


It can be utilized for multiple purposes, including statistical analysis, performance trends, program assessment, comparison of scores across the different specializations, and even predictive modeling or decision-making pertaining to curriculum development and student support.
